History of Lulu's Plant Nursery
Lulu's Plant Nursery is a family owed Plant Nursery ran by a Mexican Woman who adores plants. Lulu's plant nursery was founded in 2019 and initially began with a nursery at home. Now Lulu's Plant Nursery is a staple for locals to pick up beautiful plants.
